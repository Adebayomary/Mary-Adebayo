About Me

Dr. Mary Adebayo is a Senior Lecturer and the Head, Department of English and Literary Studies in Bingham University, Karu. She earned her Ph.D. in Literature from the University of Ibadan. Her research interest spans Postcolonial Literature, Environmental Humanities, Women's Writings, and Diasporic Literature, reflecting her passion and interest in the complexities of identity, culture, and the human experience. As an educator, Dr. Adebayo fosters academic and personal growth in her students through mentorship, coaching, and creating a stimulating learning environment that encourages critical thinking and intellectual growth. Her experience gained from many years of teaching, coaching, and researching has enabled her to achieve this feat. In addition to her teaching and administrative responsibilities, Dr. Adebayo is a prolific researcher and academic who actively participates in conferences, webinars, and seminars, advancing knowledge in her field. Furthermore, her commitment impacting and mentoring extends to coaching students in outreach initiatives that support orphanage homes and promote social responsibility. Beyond academia, Dr. Adebayo is a scholar of diverse interests. She is an avid reader, a lover of music, arts, nature and documentaries on space. Consequently, her diverse personality and scholarly expertise have earned her a revered place in academic and social circles.
